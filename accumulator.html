Accumulator Design

300V system for powering our EV vehicle

Project Overview

This accumulator will power our car's motor. At peak output, it will produce 170 amps. We are monitoring the temperature of 100 cells using thermistors and the Battery Management System via CAN communication. The thermistors measure between -41 and 100℃. Collaborated on the design of the accumulator enclosure to meet strict FSAE safety regulations. water-resistant to allow airflow for cooling without letting water into the enclosure. Designed and implemented a custom accumulator charging profile and connection to the emergency shutdown circuit to ensure safe, reliable function. developed TSMP (tractive system measuring points) system by designing an enclosure for the HV connections in Fusion, selecting HV-rated parts, and installing HV-rated resistors to prevent arcing in the event of a short.

Key Features

  • Temperature monitoring of 100 cells
  • Voltage monitoring using cell taps
  • Air cooled
  • Emergency shutdown circuit
